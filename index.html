JD POWER Award — Cinematic Intro

목표: 수상 공신력·권위를 강조하는 8–12초 인트로.

(영상 임베드 자리 — YouTube/Vimeo iframe)

Prompt

Create a cinematic intro video emphasizing the prestige and authority of the JD Power Award.
Open with a dark elegant background and floating golden particles.
Reveal 3D metallic letters "JD POWER" one-by-one (J→D→P→O→W→E→R), each with a dramatic light burst and impact.
At full reveal, sweep a golden shine across the letters to symbolize excellence and credibility.
Style: cinematic, grand, premium. Mood: inspiring, authoritative, celebratory.
Duration: 8–12s. Colors: black / deep blue / gold. Avoid playful styles.
Cartoon CEO — Opening Speech

실사 재현 없이, 웹툰/카툰풍 CEO 아바타가 축사를 전하는 10–15초 영상.

Prompt

Create a short 2D animated video in a clear cartoon style.
A CEO-like character, colorful and stylized, stands on a stage and delivers a congratulatory speech for an AI innovation event.
Bright animated conference hall background with banners “AI Innovation Celebration”.
Bold outlines, simplified details, vibrant colors. Expressive gestures and friendly tone.
Avoid photo-realism. Duration: 10–15s. Keywords: cartoon, anime-inspired, webtoon, professional.
Music Prompt — Letter Accents

세련된 시네마틱 음악. 각 문자 발화 시 악센트.

Create a modern, cinematic, and sophisticated background track to celebrate an award achievement.
Use orchestral strings, piano, and subtle electronic layers.
Each time a key letter (J, D, P, O, W, E, R) is spoken, emphasize with a sharp accent (short orchestral hit, deep drum impact, subtle electronic swell).
Avoid cheesy/folk/dance or retro-comedic melodies.
Mood: premium, refined, powerful. Tempo: ~110 BPM. Duration: 60–90s.
